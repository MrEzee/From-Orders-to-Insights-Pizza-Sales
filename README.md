# From-Orders-to-Insights-Pizza-Sales
**Project Description:**

This project explores a year’s worth of sales data from a fictitious pizza restaurant. The dataset, provided in multiple CSV files (orders, order_details, pizzas, and pizza_types), captures every order placed, the pizzas served, their sizes, prices, and ingredients. A data dictionary is included to describe each field.
The goal of this project is to transform raw transactional data into actionable insights about customer behavior, sales performance, and menu optimization.

**Key Objectives**
- Data Integration: Merge the four datasets into a single clean DataFrame for analysis.
- Revenue Analysis: Calculate total sales, monthly revenue trends, and peak sales periods.
- Order Insights: Determine total orders, busiest days of the week, and peak hours of operation.
- Menu Performance: Identify top-selling pizzas, underperforming items, and category-level performance (Classic, Chicken, Supreme, Veggie).
- Customer Preferences: Explore pizza size popularity and ingredient trends.
- Visualization: Present findings through clear charts and graphs for easy interpretation.

**Analytical Highlights**
- Total Revenue: Computed by multiplying quantity × price across all orders.
- Total Orders & Quantity Sold: Summarized to understand overall business scale.
- Peak Hours & Days: Identified busiest times for sales, useful for staffing and promotions.
- Monthly Trends: Seasonal patterns revealed, with noticeable peaks and dips.
- Top & Bottom Performers: Classic pizzas dominate sales, while niche Supreme or Veggie pizzas show lower demand.

**Tools & Libraries**
- Pandas for data cleaning and merging.
- Matplotlib and Seaborn for visualization.
- Jupyter Notebook for interactive exploration and reporting.

**Outcomes**
- This analysis provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, helping answer critical business questions:
- Which pizzas generate the most revenue?
- When are customers most likely to order?
- Which menu items may need promotion or removal?
- How do seasonal trends affect sales?
  
The insights can guide menu optimization, marketing strategies, and operational planning for a pizza restaurant.
